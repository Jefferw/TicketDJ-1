Capstone Project Summary

The Capstone project aims to develop a comprehensive helpdesk application designed to streamline and enhance customer support services. The application will provide a centralized platform for users to submit support tickets. The goal is to improve response times, resolve issues effectively, and enhance overall customer satisfaction through a user-friendly and responsive helpdesk system.
